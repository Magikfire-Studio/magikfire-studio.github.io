About Me

Stephanie is the artist, writer, and creator of Dreamwalker on WEBTOON Originals and Path of the Twinned Tailed Beast on WEBTOON Canvas. A varied career, starting with Product Design degree to UI/UX HMI Designer with Technical Artist in leading 3D technologies then finally jumping to Graphics Novels for 5 years. Producing her lifelong aspiring titles with graphics novels as the main medium. Her bold visuals, dream-like world-building and vibrant colours depict all her vivid dreams, weaving them into an exciting adventure!
